# 🎁 FNP Sales Data Analysis using Excel

## 📌 Overview
An interactive Excel sales dashboard analyzing order and revenue data for FNP (Ferns N Petals), a gifting and floral e-commerce brand. 
The dashboard tracks revenue trends, product performance, customer behavior, and regional sales across occasions like Anniversary, Birthday, Diwali, Holi, Raksha Bandhan, and Valentine's Day.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (PivotTables, PivotCharts, Slicers, Formulas)

## 📊 Key Metrics
- **Total Revenue:** ₹35,20,984.00
- **Total Orders:** 1,000
- **Avg. Order-to-Delivery Time:** 5.53 days
- **Avg. Customer Spend:** ₹3,520.98

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- **Top 5 Products by Revenue** – Best-performing gift items
- **Revenue by Category** – Cake, Colors, Mugs, Plants, Raksha Bandhan, Soft Toys, Sweets
- **Revenue by Occasion** – Anniversary, Birthday, Diwali, Holi, Raksha Bandhan, Valentine's Day
- **Revenue by Day of Week & Month** – Identifies seasonal and weekly demand patterns
- **Top 10 Cities by Orders** – Regional demand distribution
- **Interactive Slicers** – Filter by Occasion, Order Date, and Delivery Date

## 💡 Key Insights
- Colors and Soft Toys/Sweets categories together drive the majority of revenue
- Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan are the highest revenue-generating occasions
- Clear seasonal revenue spikes align with festival months
- Order volume is concentrated in a handful of top cities, useful for regional inventory planning
